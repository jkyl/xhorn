AM model prediction with frequency switching

Jonathan Kyl

Intro

I found the FWHM of the two ozone lines in our band using AM. We want to switch the frequency of the LO in order to subtract out standing waves reflected off the IF output of the mixer, but we also don't want to lose integration time on the lines. A switch of 250 MHz down in LO frequency (~5x the O3 linewidth) looks to be enough to preserve the shape of the lines in the differenced spectrum without shifting them out of band. We could also safely go in the other direction, because the shape of the BP and any contamination e.g. reflections, sattelites, also gets shifted with the LO because it's introduced before the mixer..
