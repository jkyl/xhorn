August 21, 2015 - X-Horn Beam Profile Measurement From LASR Rooftop

Comparison of our vs. Princeton's data for X-Horn at 11 GHz. Angle values for our own data have been translated ~0.5 deg using the gaussian best-fit parameter for peak position. We see a slightly tighter first sidelobe, and reflections beginning to dominate at ~80 deg (consistent with our suspicions during the data run).
